Welcome

The GNU EWE (Educating With Experience), pronounced “The g’New You,” is a Linux-focused open-source project designed to help users learn by doing. Through a combination of web tools, scripts, and hands-on guides, GNU EWE encourages exploration, experimentation, and mastery of Linux systems.

Whether you’re configuring a server, developing scripts, or managing a web interface, this project empowers you to transform your skills and embrace the philosophy of “The g’New You.”
